THOMAS LEE

A promising, soon-to-be web developer, I am currently a student in the Digital Crafts part-time web development bootcamp.

Early Life
I was born and raised in the island of Guam, a U.S. island territory in Micronesia, in the Western Pacific. After graduating from high school, I set my sights on studying computer science at Georgia Tech and left my tiny island home for the city of Atlanta.

Finding Web Development
After 5 grueling years of studying, I graduated with a bachelor's in computer science. Upon graduation, I found a career in information technology as a database administrator. I later met my wife, had a child and found a quiet life in the suburbs. But something was missing... I felt a need to find a career doing something that was creative and new. Web development seemed like a perfect career path, as I already had knowledge in the subject and experience in an adjacent field. I knew I would enjoy re-learning coding with all the advancements in web technology. I found Digital Crafts after doing much research and reaching out to some alumni. I am looking forward to my journey and I am confident I will find a new career as a web developer.
